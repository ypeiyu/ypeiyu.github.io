Peiyu Yang

I am a PhD student at the the University of Western Australia advised by Prof Ajmal Mian, A/Prof Naveed Akhtar, and A/Prof Zeyi Wen. I received my master degree from Northeastern University supervised by Prof. Lu Wang . My research interests include explainable artificial intelligence, robust model training and adversarial attacks.
